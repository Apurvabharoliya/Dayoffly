Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a leave request?
To submit a leave request, go to the "Leave Applications" section in the main menu, click on "New Request", fill out the required information including leave type, dates, and reason, then click "Submit".
How do I check my leave balance?
Your leave balance is displayed on your Dashboard. For a detailed breakdown, go to "Reports & Analytics" and select "Leave Balance Report".
What should I do if my leave request is denied?
If your leave request is denied, you will receive a notification with the reason. You can discuss this with your manager or contact HR for further clarification.
How far in advance should I submit a leave request?
We recommend submitting leave requests at least 2 weeks in advance for planned leaves. For emergencies, you can submit a request as soon as possible and notify your manager.
